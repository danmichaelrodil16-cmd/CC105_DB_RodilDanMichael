About Us

The CCS Department at Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa is dedicated to keeping students, faculty, and the university community informed about the latest developments in computer technology education. Our platform publishes timely news, event updates, and breakthroughs within the Colleges of Computer Technology at Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa.

Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa prides itself on fostering innovation, research, and excellence in computer science and technology studies. We aim to connect students and educators with relevant information and opportunities that enhance learning and career readiness in the fast-evolving tech landscape.
